History of R&J sourcing LTD

Founded by JAFUR AHAMMAD and also he is leading as a managing director/CEO, their business acumen is the grooming result of generations of entrepreneurship. R&J Sourcing Ltd opened its door to the apparel manufacturing business in 2011 as an independent vertically integrated Denim garment.Since its inception, R&J has gone from strength to strength in becoming a leading apparel manufacturer in Bangladesh. R&J has positioned itself at the forefront of the apparel manufacturing sector by utilizing a combination of innovation and visionary leadership. Today it is adding value to the country’s economy by bringing millions of dollars in business from foreign markets and acting as a flag bearer for the ‘Made in Bangladesh’ brand.
